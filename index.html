About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shuiguang Deng with co-advisors Profs. Hailiang Zhao and Xueyan Tang (NTU). Previously, I was affiliated with Alibaba Cloud, working on distributed systems and congestion control algorithms. My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of robust decision-making across generalized AI+X domains, with a particular emphasis on algorithmic design.

Research

Selected Publications

[NeurIPS 2025] Robustifying Learning-Augmented Caching Efficiently without Compromising 1-Consistency
Peng Chen, Hailiang Zhao, Jiaji Zhang, Xueyan Tang, Yixuan Wang, Shuiguang Deng
Abstract: This paper proposes a lightweight framework named Guard that efficiently improves the robustness of a broad class of learning-augmented caching algorithms while preserving their 1-consistency.
[NeurIPS 2024] Learning-Augmented Algorithms for the Bahncard Problem
Hailiang Zhao, Xueyan Tang, Peng Chen, and Shuiguang Deng
Abstract: This paper introduces PFSUM, a learning-augmented algorithm that leverages both historical data and short-term future information to enhance online decision-making.
