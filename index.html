About

I am a researcher interested in enhancing decision robustness in the AI+X domain. My previous work focuses on the intersection of learning-augmented algorithms and computer systems, bridging theoretical guarantees with practical performance. I am currently a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shuiguang Deng, and co-advised by Hailiang Zhao and Xueyan Tang. Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Soochow University and my M.S. from Kyoto University.
