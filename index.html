About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shuiguang Deng with co-advisors Profs. Hailiang Zhao and Xueyan Tang (NTU). Previously, I worked at Alibaba Cloud, focusing on distributed systems and congestion control algorithms. My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of robust decision-making in generalized AI+X domains, particularly through algorithmic design.
